## Hook variations to offer

Always give the user 2-3 so they can A/B test. Mix patterns:

- **Metric/ROI:** "This is probably the [skill] that's directly made me the most money."
- **Build-in-public:** "I built an [agent/workflow] that [does Y] while I sleep. Here's the whole thing 👇"
- **Contrarian:** "Everyone's [common approach]. I do the opposite — and it [outcome]."
- **Curiosity gap:** "I turned [boring input] into [valuable output] with one [skill]. Breakdown below."
- **Loss-framed:** "If you're still doing [manual thing] by hand, this [skill] will save you [time]."

## Qualifier snippets (for credibility)

Drop one of these near the CTA or in the final thread tweet when claims could read as hype:

- "Tested in my workflow; results vary by prompt quality."
- "Not magic — just a repeatable process. Your mileage depends on [X]."
- "v1.1 — still iterating. Feedback welcome."
